  nothing else pushes. So "last message" == "the garbled turn" there. The role
  guard exists only for the edge where the garble has empty content and nothing
  was appended; in that case the last message is a user/tool_result turn and must
  not be dropped.

  it is a cheap pivot. The Maker should build whichever the supervisor confirms.

- **The garble reproducer is `makeEndTurnStream` with `stop_reason: tool_use`.**

  shape (content message, no tool_use block). `makeGarbledToolUseStream` in the
