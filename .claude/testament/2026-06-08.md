## Why ci:fix alone wasn't enough

`pnpm ci:fix` (`biome check --diagnostic-level=error --fix`) applies only safe fixes. The `useBlockStatements` fixes are classified as "Unsafe" by Biome (they alter AST structure, even though they're purely stylistic). `pnpm exec biome check --write --unsafe` scoped to the 3 affected source files applied them. The mission explicitly sanctions `pnpm exec biome check --write` scoped to the changed files as an acceptable alternative.

## NE3 fix reasoning

The dead suppression and `as any` were placed on different lines — the comment was on the `const actual = call(...)` opening line (line 1337), but `as any` was on the closing `}` line (line 1349). Biome reported the suppression as "has no effect" because there was no `noExplicitAny` diagnostic on the line the comment targeted. The fix was to remove both — the NE3 input is valid TypeScript, so `as any` was never needed.
