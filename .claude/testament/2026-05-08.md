## mcp-exec backfill

When mcp-exec was migrated from its standalone repo, the standalone history (preview.1-6) was not seeded into changes.jsonl. Phase 3 backfilled it: 19 entries converted from the standalone CHANGELOG and prepended before the existing entries.
